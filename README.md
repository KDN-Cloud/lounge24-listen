## 🎼 What We Play

The rotation pulls from a pretty wide range of stuff. Expect anything from:

- **House** / Deep House / House Misc
- **Jungle** / Drum & Bass
- **Trip Hop**
- **Trance**
- **Dubstep**
- **Jazz**
- **Reggae** / Dub
- **Classical**
- **Psytrance / Psy**
- **Metal / Rock**
- **Lounge / Chill**
- **Legacy Recorded Mixes** (old recorded sets from over the years)

It's a mixed bag on purpose. The vibe shifts throughout the day.
